About Me

I'm a tattoo artist, jewelry designer, and all around artistic person. My current passion is designing and making glass seed bead earrings with porcupine quills, buckskin and gemstones. I love traditional Native American beaded earrings and learned how to bead from someone who is of Ute heritage. I sell my work at art fairs and online and will do custom work. I’m currently based in Colorado.

Lots of styles!

Bright colors, earth tones, down to your shoulders or short loops - you choose the length and color scheme!

Lots of colors!

Classic glass seed beads on their own, combined with porcupine quills and/or gemstones. More silver or less. You choose!

About the Earrings

My earrings come in a variety of lengths and colors and can include porcupine quills, metal cones, buckskin and gemstones. The images on this website are illustrative only since the exact earrings may have already been sold. Prices vary based on the complexity of design and if gemstones are included, but range from $20 - $45.
